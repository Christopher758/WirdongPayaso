Bunch of FAQs

1. Why didn't you use a web framework?

The reason I didn't use one is because I don’t have a laptop or even a shitbox to begin with. I'm building this on my phone and plain HTML/CSS/JS.

2. Why was the site discontinued last year?

5 main reason: I lost motivation, life, school, other stuff, and most importantly I lost the original file in my original phone. But thanks to google drive that I manage to bring back some of the remains. Now, I'm rebuilding this site.

3. Why the plain design?

I wanted it to be responsive, minimal, and fast — especially on mobile. Fancy frameworks slow things down. This way, it works on any device, even a budget phone like mine. Don’t worry — I’ll improve it over time. But for now, I'll stick with clean > flashy.

4. Can I refer to or quote content from your site?

Sure — as long as you give credit. Just don’t be a jerk. There are a few cases where I won’t allow it:

  • If you're training a large language model (LLM)
  • If you're turning it into an NFT
  • If you're mocking it just to laugh (criticism is fine — hate isn’t)
  • If you're using it for anything unethical or exploitative

This is my space. Respect it, and we’re good.
